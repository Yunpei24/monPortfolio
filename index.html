Vice winner of the 2nd edition of the P.A.S Challenge in 2023

For this 2nd edition of the challenge, I joined the girls' team in proposing a solution to the problem of reforestation proposed by the UNDP. Our team set up an application based on AI, specifically Computer Vision, which can map a well-defined area in order to monitor changes in vegetation cover and assess carbon credits.

The winning team in the P.A.S Challenge 1st edition in 2022

The P.A.S (One Problem, One Algorithm, One Solution) challenge in 2022, the 1st edition of which was organised by the IAS (Institut des Algorithmes du Sénégal) in partnership with the UNDP and the AUF, was a competition aimed at proposing innovative solutions based on Artificial Intelligence to solve problems in areas such as health, transport, education, agriculture and many others. Our team worked on transcribing the medical notes of health professionals in French and English, as well as in local languages (Wolof, Pular, Fongbe). As a result, we have developed a complete tool enabling healthcare professionals to transcribe, translate, edit and print digital prescriptions.
